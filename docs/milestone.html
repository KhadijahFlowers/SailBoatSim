Final Project: Boat Simulator

Milestone Status Report

Khadijah Flowers, Heesoo Jang, Anais Miller



Overview of Accomplishments

So far, we have laid the ground works for each of the aspects of our project. We designed our GUI with all the components we need to manipulate the sailboat. This includes changing the boat type apperance, altering the wind speed and sun light amounts, and changing the density frame/s of simulation rendering. We have also added in the keyboard commands to move the sailboat around in the North, South, East, West directions.

The next part we have accomplished is designing our boat, sail, and water plane. The boat was created using OpenGL. The sail is able to move with the wind so if we increase the wind intensity, the sail will puff out more and flap around less. The water plane is created by having a normal plane and adding a shader on top of it to project the feeling of water.

Progress and Plan Updates

Our working schedule plan changed. Instead of working on one part at a time each week, we divided up the parts and are each tackling one of the parts.

We also changed the scope of the project. Based on feedback from the project proposal, we decided that we will focus on the motion, sail, and wind simulator. For the water simulator, we are instead using a shader to give it the water texture. To create the water movement, we are going to make it like the pinned cloth and have the water cloth move accordingly to the amount of wind.
